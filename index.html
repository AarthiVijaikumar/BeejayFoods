Meet Bejay

We are content that Bejay foods is a House of Brands of Ganesh & Co. We coined the name “Bejay” in the honour of Balagurusamy and Jeyalakshmi. To promote healthy and traditional food intakes in a materialistic society, Bejay was founded in 2022. To make the job easy for you, we manufacture whole wheat & multigrain atta and millets products.

Our vision is to regain forgotten healthy food habits and preserve this for future generations. Millets and traditional rice have more nutritional benefits than regular rice and wheat products.

To attain our vision, we are now progressing towards cold-pressed oil, other millet products and flours. We are delighted that our products receive glowing reviews everywhere.
